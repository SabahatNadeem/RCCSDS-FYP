Video Analytics

The feature of Video Analytics help the admin to examine the situation of SOPs violations in a particular area and location through different types of graphs. The Admin is also able to apply different filters of time and type of graph in order to get better insights

Report Generation

The Feature of Report Generator enables the admin to generate daily,monthly or yearly report about the SOPs violations and the no of safe and unsafe people in a specific view.

Real Time Monitoring

Real Time Monitoring enables to monitor video at a particular instant in order to get the idea about the no of people and the proportion of safe and unsafe individuals in Real time

Sending Emails

The Emails regarding the insights generated per day are send to the respective important members of the organization.This is the responsibility of the admin.
